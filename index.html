Privacy Policy for Bingo Super Star

Bingo Super Star (the "Game") is a mobile bingo game designed to provide an enjoyable gaming experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how we collect and use your information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information we receive from users of the Game.

When you use the Game, we may collect certain information about you, including:

Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account to use the Game. We may also collect personal information from you when you contact us for support or submit feedback. This personal information may be used to create and manage your account, provide customer support, respond to your inquiries and requests, and send you promotional offers and marketing communications. We may also use your personal information to communicate with you about updates to the Game or changes to our policies.

Usage Information: We may collect information about your use of the Game, such as your IP address, device type, and browser type. We may also collect information about the pages you view within the Game, the time and date of your visit, and the duration of your visit. This usage information may be used to analyze how you interact with the Game, including which features you use and how often you use them. This helps us understand how to improve the Game and personalize your experience. We may also use this information to detect and prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

Payment Information: If you make purchases through the Game, we may collect your payment information, such as your credit card number or PayPal account information. This payment information may be used to process your transactions and complete your purchases. We may also use your payment information to prevent fraud or other illegal activities.

Game Data: We may collect data about your gaming activity, such as the types of bingo games you play, the duration of your gameplay, and your in-game purchases. We may also collect data about your device, such as your operating system and device identifier. This game data may be used to analyze your gameplay and improve the Game. For example, we may use this data to create new game features, improve existing features, or identify trends in player behavior. We may also use this data to personalize your experience with the Game and suggest games or in-game purchases that may be of interest to you.

We use this information to:

Operate and improve the Game;

Communicate with you about the Game;

Personalize your experience with the Game;

Provide customer support;

Process your payments;

Comply with legal and regulatory requirements; and

Enforce our terms of service.

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Game, such as payment processors, data analytics providers, and customer support providers. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information in response to a legal request, such as a subpoena, court order, or search warrant, or to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country.
